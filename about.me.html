Meet Izzy!

Born and raised in Central New York, It was an ideal day for me to spend
my time outdoors enjoying the constant changing of the world around me. Growing up in CNY
helped me to develop a strong appreciation for the changing of seasons,
the critters that venture out of hiding at dusk and dawn,
and the unique weather patterns.
Throught my time in school I often caught myself daydreaming and doodling, always curious of
what the world was like outside of what I knew. I constantly craved learning and experince; often what was
not provided in my traditional public school. I worked hard. I pursued my interest and appreciation of the world
through Art meanwhile keeping my grades up in hopes that one day I could shape my interests into a career as well.
Currently I am first year at SUNY New Paltz majoring in Visual Arts and planning to get a BFA in Painting!
Art is truly my passion, I hope I can utilize my dedication to it to help me live life to the fullest!
